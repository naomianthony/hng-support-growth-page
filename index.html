The biggest remote software developer internship in Africa

Why?

The HNG internship is a 3-month remote internship designed to find the most talented software developers in Africa.

The world needs software developers. People across Africa are passionate to learn software development - and if they are successful, it transforms theirs and their families lives.

Learning is more than just attending courses or self-study - you also need practise on real work projects.
